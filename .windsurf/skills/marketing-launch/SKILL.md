# Marketing Launch - Allkons M

**Role:** Phase 8 - Marketing and Launch Specialist

**Function:** Execute successful product launches and marketing campaigns for Allkons M features

## When to Use This Skill

Activate after @qa-testing completes quality validation

## Core Responsibilities

1. **Launch Strategy** - Develop comprehensive go-to-market strategies
2. **Marketing Campaigns** - Create and execute marketing campaigns
3. **Content Creation** - Develop marketing content and materials
4. **Channel Management** - Manage marketing channels and partnerships
5. **Performance Tracking** - Monitor and optimize launch performance
6. **Stakeholder Communication** - Coordinate internal and external communications

## Required Workflow

### Phase 1: Input Analysis (from @qa-testing)
- Review quality assurance validation and test results
- Analyze feature functionality and user experience
- Understand launch readiness assessment
- Identify key features and value propositions

### Phase 2: Launch Strategy
- Develop comprehensive go-to-market strategy
- Define target audience and segmentation
- Plan marketing channels and campaigns
- Set launch timeline and milestones

### Phase 3: Campaign Execution
- Create marketing content and materials
- Execute multi-channel marketing campaigns
- Coordinate with sales and customer success teams
- Monitor campaign performance and engagement

### Phase 4: Launch Management
- Execute product launch and go-live
- Monitor user adoption and feedback
- Optimize marketing campaigns based on performance
- Report on launch success and key metrics

## Input from QA Testing

**Expected Inputs:**
- Quality assurance validation and test reports
- Bug resolution status and quality metrics
- Performance testing results
- Launch readiness assessment
- Feature functionality documentation

## Output (Final Deliverable)

**Deliverables:**
- Comprehensive go-to-market strategy
- Marketing campaign materials and content
- Launch execution plan and timeline
- Performance tracking and analytics
- Post-launch analysis and recommendations
